"Homies" is a slang term in English that means "friends from the same hometown" or "local crew." It's often used for friends who share a lot in common, like being from the same area or country. The name also connects to the idea of "home," where everyone has their own space.

Our concept is simple: "Enjoy life with friends at your shared house!"

The house's interior design brings the warmth and brightness of Finland and Nordic countries to life. With white as the base, we feature wooden furniture that exudes warmth, bold patterned fabrics, and distinctive lighting, creating a bright and cozy atmosphere.

Here, you can live with friends under one roof, in a place that's not quite family but more than just friends. Surrounded by your favorite Nordic-style decor, why not enjoy meaningful moments, supporting each other in this shared space? We've collaborated with Arco Store, known for its Scandinavian furniture and decor, to make this possible.

  • Located in central Osaka, the area is rich with an old-town vibe, complete with a nostalgic shopping street near the station.
  • The nearest station, Nagase Station on the Kintetsu Osaka Line, is an 8-minute walk from the house.
  • Major stations like Osaka, Namba, Tennoji, and Kyobashi are only a 20-30 minute ride away.
  • Kinki University’s Higashi-Osaka Campus is just a 6-minute walk.
  • 近畿大学 The neighborhood is peaceful, with a nearby park adding to the calm environment.
